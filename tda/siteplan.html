Site Plan

Site Name:

Blake City Department of Agriculture - This name represents a department of agriculture website for the fictional town of Blake City.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide updates from the Department of Agriculure as well as to list the active licenses associated with the Department. The site will also allow individuals to apply for a license with the Department.

Scenarios:

What are the different license types available?
